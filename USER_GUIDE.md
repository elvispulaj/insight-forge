# InsightForge User Guide

**InsightForge** is an AI-powered Business Intelligence (BI) assistant designed to transform raw data into actionable strategic insights. By combining the power of Large Language Models (LLMs) with intuitive visual dashboards, InsightForge allows users to analyze complex datasets, generate professional visualizations, and ask questions about their business in plain English.

### 1. Getting Started
- **Log In**: Access the secure platform using your credentials.
- **Connect AI**: Navigate to the sidebar and enter your **OpenAI API Key**. This key unlocks the advanced AI analysis and chat features.
- **Upload Data**: Use the **"Data Source"** panel in the sidebar to upload your business files.
  - **Supported Formats**: CSV, Excel, JSON, PDF, DOCX, and Text files.
  - *Tip: If you don't have data ready, select "Sample Dataset" to explore the app's capabilities.*

### 2. Navigating the Dashboard
Once logged in, the **Dashboard** serves as your mission control.
- **KPI Cards**: Instantly view critical metrics like Total Revenue, Average Sales, or file statistics.
- **Quick Access**: Use the central grid to jump to specific tools like **AI Analysis**, **Visualizations**, or **Q&A**.
